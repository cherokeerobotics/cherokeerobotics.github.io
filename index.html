Our Story

Our Story

In 2019, a small band of CHS Warriors were sitting in a physics class when their teacher expressed his desire to pioneer a robotics team at his school. Unfortunately, he was not able to sponsor the team, but the members persevered! They are now looking for sponsors, working towards getting a robot made, and striving towards making the robotics team the best that it can possibly be and hoping to leave an amazing club in the hands of future students.
